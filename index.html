11/11 補充單字

As well as 等同/也

He was suffering from shock as well as the after-effects of drugs.

她在毒品副作用發作的同時還引發了休克。

Messenger 信使/通訊兵

The receptionist recognized him at once.

接待員一眼就認出她了。

Annoying 討厭的/惱人的

The annoying thing about the scheme is that it’s confusing.

這項計畫讓人討厭的地方是它讓人一頭霧水。

Put someone through 打給某個人

Can you put me through to customer services,please?

請幫我打給某個人好嗎?

Stuttgart 斯圖卡特

I took the fast train to Stuttgart at 27th.

我乘坐二十七號的的晚間快車到斯圖卡特。

Banquet 宴會/請客/酒席

He entertained me with a banquet.

他以宴會款待我們。
